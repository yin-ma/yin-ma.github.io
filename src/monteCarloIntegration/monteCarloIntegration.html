Monte Carlo Integration

$$I = \int_{a}^{b} f(x) {dx} = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=0}^N\frac{f(x_{i})}{p(x_{i})}$$

let's look at an example:

$$\int_{0}^{2} x^{2}dx$$

Analytical solution: 2.666...

Numerical solution: 0

now try to use importance sampling where pdf = 3x^2/8

Analytical solution: 2.666...

Numerical solution: 0
